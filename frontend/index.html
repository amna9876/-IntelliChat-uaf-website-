About UAF

Established in 1906, UAF is Pakistan's oldest agricultural university. Spread over 1,200 acres, we nurture future leaders in agriculture, science, and technology.

Our commitment to innovation and community service drives agricultural transformation across Pakistan.

Our Mission

To advance knowledge in agriculture through quality education and research for food security.

Our Vision

To be a world-class university contributing to global agricultural sustainability.

UAF Campus Events

Celebrate culture, agriculture, and innovation at UAF's iconic annual festivals.

🌾 Agricultural

Gur Mela

UAF's annual jaggery festival celebrating agricultural heritage since decades. Features live demonstrations, stalls, and cultural performances.

🌿 Agricultural

Kapas Mela

The Cotton Festival showcases UAF's cutting-edge cotton research and farming culture with expert talks and field exhibitions.

🏞️ Cultural

Wadiyain Mela

A vibrant cultural mela representing the valley traditions and regional heritage of Punjab, organized by UAF students.

🌸 Spring

Jashn-e-Bahara

UAF's flagship Spring Festival — the most awaited annual event featuring music, art, food stalls, and student competitions.

⚽ Sports

UAF Sports Gala

Annual inter-department sports competition — cricket, football, tennis, swimming, and athletics across UAF's world-class Sports Complex.

💻 Technology

TechFest UAF

Annual technology festival by UAF Computing Society. Hackathons, AI/ML demos, robotics, drone shows, and a career expo every October.
